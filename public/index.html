ABOUT

The "Zuper Supir" driving school has existed since 1990 and is the first private driving school in Koja, North Jakarta. which also makes her the oldest. There are a large number of candidates, both younger and older past through our training very successfully and became safe road traffic drivers. This year we are celebrating our 32th birthday and during all these years we managed to raise the school to enviable level, which is best evidenced by the large number of clients who left very satisfied and well dressed. Many compliments arrive at our address every day and we really think they are candidates the best evaluators, and the fact is that most clients come to take their exams with us what they confirm large groups at each passing of both the theoretical and practical part. However, we are open to criticism and we would be happy to change something for the good of you and our team.
